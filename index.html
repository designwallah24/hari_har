Our Story

The passion behind every sandwich

👥

Abhishek Sahu

Founder & Chef

जयश्री सांवरिया सेठ
❤️

What started as a passion project has become Bhopal's most beloved street food destination. Harihar Sandwich is more than just food – it's an experience that brings smiles to faces and warmth to hearts.

❤️

With dedication, hygiene, and a unique recipe that's been perfected over time, we've earned the love of thousands of customers. Every sandwich is prepared with care, loaded with premium ingredients, and served with a smile.

❤️

From students to families, from late-night cravings to celebration treats, Harihar Sandwich has become a part of Bhopal's food culture. Our journey is fueled by your love and support, and we promise to keep serving you the best sandwiches in town!
